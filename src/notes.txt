CONWAY GAME OF LIFE SIMULATOR:

0 = dead;
1 = alive;

RULES:
1 = underpopulation:LIVE cells with less than 2 live neighbors => DIE
2 = live on: LIVE cells with 2 - 3 live neighbors LIVE;
3 = overpopulation: LIVE cells with GREATER than 3 neighbors => DIE;
4 = reproduction: DEAD cells with exactly 3 neighbors => LIVE (reborn);
